ABOUT HØJBJERG

The peaceful, leafy area and
former settlement of Højbjerg is
famous for the vast Marselisborg
Forest – a popular destination for
picnickers and cyclists – and for
the centuries-old trees around the
sandy Moesgård Beach.
And we are just a group of friends
who love to hang out in nature
together!
